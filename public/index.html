How To Play

  1. Enter a room or create one with a unique code. Share with friends!
  2. One player draws a word. Others guess via chat. Fastest guess = more points.
  3. No typing the exact word as hints in your drawing.
  4. Be respectful: no spam, foul language, or revealing answers outside the game.
  5. Six rounds per game. At the end, see who’s the top guesser and artist!
